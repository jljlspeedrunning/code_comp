What is our mission?

Our mission is to explain several ways people are able to obtain clean water and how we manage them. We will mostly talk about the efficient usage of water, and how clean water is obtained. More information can be found with the links on the top of this page.

Where does our water come from?

Oceans are currently not a reliable source of water. Many use freshwater glaciers, rivers, lakes, hot springs, aquifers, etc. Other sources may use reservoirs with proper health regulations, or the filtration of undrinkable water (such as from an ocean, wastewater treatment facility, etc.). In any way, a reliable source of drinking water (and other uses of water) is important.
