What is our mission?

Our mission is to explain the many ways people are able obtain clean water and how we manage them. More information can be found with the links on the top of the page.

Where does our water come from?

Oceans are currently not a reliable source of water. Many use freshwater glacier, rivers, lakes, hot springs, aquifers, etc. Other sources may use reservoirs with proper health regulations, or the filtration of unusable water (like from the ocean, wastewater treatment facility, etc.). In any way, a reliable source of drinking water (and other uses of water) is important.
